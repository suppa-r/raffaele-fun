Way out in left field my working career began at maybe 11 or 12yrs. old. I'm not that certain.
That job lasted only one brief summer, and it paid me $10.00 per week. Paid in rolled quarters! I'm certain of that.
I wasn't even at the teenage attitude yet and I was ruling like King of Shit Mountain. Walking taller than ten feet high. I had 10 bucks in my pocket and loudly singing Amazing Grace all the way to my Swiss Bank. Money made me rich.
I often reflect if that fast times was like a trauma that caused my immenent and Modern Gypsy Career. And Lifstyle.
I present this because, there's an important Irony here.
If we're similar, you'll find it funny too!
For 99% of my fledged Design Career, my Pay remained steady at $10.00 per week!
Paydays became easier though: I wasn't getting paid in quarters. The silver lining, if you choose the positive.
